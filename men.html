About Us

We are Mentor Men Leadership Forum, a platform which began by an heavenly and divinely orchestrated Vision and Hunger the lord laid upon our Pastor and Convener in the name of Pastor Gentle C Nwobi(Eagle).

We don't discriminate between denominations, because we believe by the provision of the Scriptures, that being a Catholic, an Anglican, a Presbetarian, or even Penticostal dosen't automatically Migrate you from the family of the Devil, to that of Christ, the Migration is attained/made possible by the provision of Salvation; Which was accesible to all when Jesus Christ the first Begotten of the father which was and is God, came down from his highly exalted throne, with the sole purpose of reconciling us back to Him and to his Father.
